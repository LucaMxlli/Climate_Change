Interesting Organisations And Movements

There are multiple organisations and social movements which’s mission is to save the environment. Most of those organisations are non-profit organisations. Non-profit organisations are organisations which don’t want to generate profit with their work. They care about something and just try to make things better without requesting any money for it.

A social movement is a loosely organized effort by a large group of people to achieve a particular goal, typically a social or political one. In the following slider you will find several examples for organisations and social movements.

Greenpeace

Greenpeace is a non-profit organisation which is know for its direct actions at places which are "destroying" the environment

Image: https://www.planet-wissen.de/natur/umwelt/umweltverschmutzung/pwiegreenpeace100.html


350.org

350.org is an international environmental organization which’s goal is to end the use of fossil fuels and transition to renewable energy by building a global, grassroots movement

Image: https://350.org/


Fridays for future

"Fridays for future" is a social nternational movement of school students who skip Friday classes to participate in demonstrations to demand action from political leaders to prevent climate change and for the fossil fuel industry to transition to renewable energy.

Image: https://www.tagesspiegel.de/politik/fridays-for-future-bewegung-dem-rad-in-die-speichen-greifen/24192222.html
